About Mumbai

In 2008, Mumbai was named an alpha world city. It has the highest number of millionaires and billionaires among all cities in India. Mumbai is home to three UNESCO World Heritage Sites: the Elephanta Caves, Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus, and the city's distinctive ensemble of Victorian and Art Deco buildings.

Few of the famous landmarks of Mumbai are

  • Gateway of India
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus
  • Taj Mahal Palace
  • Marine Drive
  • Haji Ali Dargah
  • Bombay Stock Exchange
  • Bandra Worli Sea Link
